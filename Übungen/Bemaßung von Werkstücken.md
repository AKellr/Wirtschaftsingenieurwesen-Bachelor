## 1. Dimensioning Rules (_Bemaßungsregeln_) 📏

| **Question Asked**                                                                        | **Correct Answer**                                                                                                                                                                                                    | **Source/Concept**       |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| What are the two fundamental rules regarding repetition and completeness in dimensioning? | 1. Every dimension is specified only once (_Jedes Maß wird nur einmal angegeben_) 2. Dimensioning must be complete in single-part drawings (_In Einzelteil-Zeichnungen muss die Bemaßung vollständig sein_).          | General Rules            |
| What are the four main elements of a dimension entry (_Maßangabe_)?                       | **Dimension Line** (_Maßlinie_), **Nominal Dimension Value** (_Nennmaßwert_), **Dimension Line Termination** (_Maßlinienbegrenzung_), and **Dimension Auxiliary Line** (_Maßhilfslinie_).                             | Elements of Dimensioning |
| What are the line type and spacing rules for dimension lines?                             | Must be a **narrow solid line** (_schmale Volllinie_)9. Spacing: $\sim 10 \text{ mm}$ from the edge, $\ge 7 \text{ mm}$ between lines. Auxiliary lines extend $1 \text{ to } 2 \text{ mm}$ beyond the dimension line. | Drawing Specifications   |
| What are the rules for the Nominal Dimension Value (_Nennmaßwert_)?                       | It must **not be crossed by any lines**, and the standard height (_Größe_) is **$3.5 \text{ mm}$**.                                                                                                                   | Value Placement          |
| What symbols denote diameter, radius, threads, and auxiliary dimensions?                  | **Diameter** ($\Phi$ / _Durchmesserzeichen_), **Radius** (**R**), **Threads** (**M**), and **Auxiliary Dimensions** are in **round brackets** (_runde Klammern_).                                                     | Special Symbols          |

## 2. Types of Dimensioning (_Bemaßungsarten_) and Manufacturing (_Fertigung_) 🏭

| **Question Asked**                                                 | **Correct Answer**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | **Source/Concept**          |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| What are the three main types of dimensioning?                     | **Function-related** (_Funktionsbezogen_), **Manufacturing-related** (_Fertigungsbezogen_), and **Inspection-related** (_Prüfbezogen_).                                                                                                                                             | Purpose-driven dimensioning |
| What is the main manufacturing process focused on?                 | **Separating** (_Trennen_), specifically **Machining with a geometrically defined cutting edge** (_Spanen mit geometrisch bestimmter Schneide_).                                                                                                                                    | DIN 8580 Main Group         |
| What are the three primary machining methods?                      | **Turning** (_Drehen_), **Milling** (_Fräsen_), and **Drilling** (_Bohren_)                                                                                                                                                                                                         | Machining Methods           |
| What are the kinematics (movements) for Turning (_Drehen_)?        | **Workpiece** (_Werkstück_) performs the **rotating movement** (_drehende Bewegung_). The **Tool** (_Werkzeug_) performs the **feed movement** (_Vorschubbewegung_).                                                                                                                | Turning Kinematics          |
| What are the kinematics (movements) for Drilling (_Bohren_)?       | The **Tool** (_Werkzeug_) performs both the **rotating cutting movement** (_drehende Schnittbewegung_) and the **feed movement** (_Vorschubbewegung_).                                                                                                                              | Drilling Kinematics         |
| What is the purpose of the five main drilling tools (_Werkzeuge_)? | **Twist Drill** (_Spiralbohrer_): Creates holes. **Counterbore/Countersink** (_Senker_): Creates offset holes. **Center Drill** (_Zentrierbohrer_): Guides the drill. **Tap** (_Gewindebohrer_): Creates threads. **Reamer** (_Reibahle_): Creates holes with high surface quality. | Specialized Drilling Tools  |
